About Us:

Stormcrow Drone Services is a Harrisburg-based company that specializes in real estate photography and videography.

There is no project too big or too small that the Stormcrow team would not eagerly take on. We offer year-round services with as many rain-dates as needed. Although we are located in the Harrisburg, we are willing to travel to you!



Our pilot: Nick Pachella

Nick graduated from West Chester University in May 2017 with a bachelors degree in physics. He began flying in June 2017 with his very first drone: Holy Stone X400C. After his first lift-off the leightweight drone made a B-line for the neighbors tallest tree, and there it remains, as a reminder to always read the user manual.

Many hours of practice later, Nick upgraded to his current model: DJI Mavic Air. Nick says, “If this drone were any smarter, it would do your taxes for you!”



Safety:

Nick is FAA-certified and insured for up to $10 million.

Our number one goal is and always will be safety. We ensure that weather conditions are ideal for both flight and photography/videography. Should a session be cancelled due to inclement weather, we can reschedule as many times as necessary to complete the project.
