What is the most powerful tool in the modern world? Data. Advancements in technology have allowed for better and more efficient methods of collecting data. One such method is via aerial photography and videography. Whether the data is total square footage of plots of land, crop health, a-symmetric volume measurements, or memories, getting the full picture with a bird's eye view is sure to help.
